What is restorative dentistry?
Improve beyond your teeth’s natural functions.
Restorative dentistry with Dr. Lue in New York is a branch of dentistry that focuses on restoring teeth to proper function and maintaining oral health. In restorative dentistry, restorations come in many different forms and serve various functions. Restoration will typically protect injured structures from further damage by restoring the full natural form and function of damaged structures or possibly even improving upon that.
Restorative dentistry can involve some or even all of these:
  • Operative: the treatment of diseases of teeth’s tissue, and the restoration of the function and aesthetics of tissue.
  • Periodontal: a specialized branch of dentistry that practices prevention, diagnosis, and the treatment of gum disease.
  • Endodontics: or root canal specialization, endodontics treats dental pulp and the tissue surrounding the roots of your tooth.
  • Prosthodontics: designing, manufacturing, and fitting of artificial replacements for teeth and other parts of your mouth.
Dr. Lue, a New York dentist, is expertly trained in assessing and treating oral diseases that have progressed beyond normal repair. With restorative dentistry treatments from Dr. Lue you can restore and improve your overall dental health.
